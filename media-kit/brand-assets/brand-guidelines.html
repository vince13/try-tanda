Tanda Brand Guidelines

Version 1.0 | Last Updated: December 2025

1. Brand Overview

Tanda is Africa's native video-commerce platform, revolutionizing how creators, sellers, and consumers interact with commerce through short-form video content. Our brand represents innovation, trust, and the future of mobile commerce in emerging markets.

These guidelines ensure consistent representation of the Tanda brand across all media, marketing materials, and communications.

2. Logo Usage

2.1 Logo Variations

Full Logo (Dark Background)

Use on dark backgrounds

Full Logo (Light Background)

Use on light backgrounds

Icon Only

Square icon for app icons, favicons

2.2 Logo Clear Space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the "T" in the logo mark. Never place text, images, or other elements within this clear space.

2.3 Logo Sizing

  • Minimum size: 120px width for full logo
  • Recommended size: 200px+ width for print materials
  • Icon minimum: 32x32px for favicons, 512x512px for app icons

2.4 Logo Do's and Don'ts

✓ Do

  • Use the logo with proper clear space
  • Maintain aspect ratio when resizing
  • Use appropriate logo variation for background
  • Keep logo colors consistent with brand palette

✗ Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change logo colors
  • Place logo on busy backgrounds
  • Rotate or modify the logo
  • Add effects (shadows, outlines, etc.)

3.3 Text Colors

  • Primary Text: #ffffff (White) - Main content
  • Secondary Text: #cccccc (Light Gray) - Supporting content
  • Muted Text: #888888 (Medium Gray) - Less important content

3.4 Color Usage Guidelines

  • Use the primary gradient for logos, CTAs, and key brand elements
  • Use pink (#ff0050) for primary actions and emphasis
  • Use cyan (#00f2ea) for secondary actions, links, and accents
  • Maintain sufficient contrast for accessibility (WCAG AA minimum)
  • Avoid using brand colors for large background areas (use neutrals instead)

4.3 Typography Scale

  • H1 (Headings): 3rem (48px) - 800 weight
  • H2 (Section Headings): 2rem (32px) - 700 weight
  • H3 (Subheadings): 1.5rem (24px) - 600 weight
  • Body Text: 1rem (16px) - 400 weight
  • Small Text: 0.875rem (14px) - 400 weight
  • Caption: 0.75rem (12px) - 400 weight

4.4 Typography Guidelines

  • Use Inter for all text elements
  • Maintain line height of 1.6 for body text
  • Use appropriate font weights for hierarchy
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for readability
  • Limit line length to 70-80 characters for optimal reading

5. Spacing & Layout

5.1 Spacing System

Use consistent spacing multiples of 0.5rem (8px):

  • XS: 0.5rem (8px) - Tight spacing
  • SM: 1rem (16px) - Standard spacing
  • MD: 1.5rem (24px) - Medium spacing
  • LG: 2rem (32px) - Large spacing
  • XL: 3rem (48px) - Extra large spacing

5.2 Border Radius

  • Standard: 1rem (16px) - Cards, buttons, containers
  • Small: 0.5rem (8px) - Small elements, badges
  • Large: 1.5rem (24px) - Large cards, modals

6. Brand Voice & Tone

6.1 Voice Characteristics

  • Innovative: Forward-thinking, cutting-edge
  • Trustworthy: Reliable, secure, transparent
  • Empowering: Enabling creators and sellers
  • Accessible: Clear, simple, inclusive
  • African-First: Proudly African, globally relevant

6.2 Tone Guidelines

  • Use clear, direct language
  • Avoid jargon; explain technical terms
  • Be enthusiastic but professional
  • Focus on benefits, not just features
  • Use inclusive, diverse language
