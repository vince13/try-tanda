üõ°Ô∏è Our Commitment to Safety

Tanda is committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and positive environment for all users. We believe in fostering authentic connections while maintaining the highest standards of user safety and content moderation.

üéØ CONTENT MODERATION

Automated Systems

  • AI-Powered Filtering: Advanced machine learning algorithms detect and filter inappropriate content
  • Real-time Scanning: All uploaded content is scanned before publication
  • Keyword Detection: Prohibited language and harmful content identification
  • Image Recognition: Detection of inappropriate visual content

Human Review

  • Expert Moderators: Trained content moderation team reviews flagged content
  • 24/7 Monitoring: Continuous oversight of platform activity
  • Escalation Process: Complex cases reviewed by senior moderation staff
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Moderators trained in African cultural contexts

Prohibited Content

  • Violence, graphic content, or harmful imagery
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or harassment
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Dangerous activities or self-harm
  • Spam, scams, or fraudulent content
  • Copyright infringement
  • Misinformation or fake news

üë• USER SAFETY

Age Verification

  • Minimum Age: 13+ for basic features, 18+ for full platform access
  • Age Verification: Identity verification for users 18+ accessing commerce features
  • Parental Awareness: Clear information for parents about platform features
  • Age-Appropriate Content: Content filtering based on user age

Harassment Prevention

  • Block & Report: Easy-to-use tools for blocking and reporting users
  • Anti-Bullying Measures: Proactive detection of bullying behavior
  • Safe Spaces: Community guidelines promoting respectful interactions
  • Support Resources: Links to mental health and safety resources

Privacy Controls

  • Profile Privacy: Granular privacy settings for user profiles
  • Content Visibility: Control over who can see posts and content
  • Location Privacy: Optional location sharing with privacy controls
  • Data Control: Users can download, modify, or delete their data

üë∂ CHILD SAFETY

Protection Measures

  • COPPA Compliance: Full compliance with Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
  • Age-Appropriate Content: Strict filtering for users under 18
  • Parental Controls: Tools for parents to monitor and control child accounts
  • Educational Resources: Safety education for young users and parents

Reporting Mechanisms

  • Quick Report: One-tap reporting for safety concerns
  • Anonymous Reporting: Option to report without revealing identity
  • Priority Response: Expedited review of child safety reports
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: Collaboration with authorities when necessary

Content Restrictions

  • No Direct Messaging: Limited messaging for users under 16
  • Supervised Accounts: Enhanced monitoring for younger users
  • Educational Content: Focus on positive, educational content
  • Community Mentorship: Positive role models and mentors

üîí PRIVACY & DATA PROTECTION

Data Security

  • End-to-End Encryption: Secure messaging and data transmission
  • Secure Storage: Industry-standard data protection measures
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security assessments
  • Incident Response: Rapid response to security breaches

User Rights

  • Data Portability: Users can export their data
  • Right to Deletion: Complete account and data removal
  • Consent Management: Clear consent for data collection and use
  • Transparency: Clear privacy policy and data practices

Compliance

  • GDPR Compliance: European data protection standards
  • Local Regulations: Compliance with African data protection laws
  • Industry Standards: Following best practices for social platforms
  • Regular Updates: Continuous improvement of privacy measures

üì¢ REPORTING & ENFORCEMENT

How to Report

  1. In-App Reporting: Use the report button on any content or user
  2. Email Reporting: Send detailed reports to safety@tanda.media
  3. Help Center: Submit reports through our help center
  4. Emergency Contact: Use emergency@tanda.media for urgent safety issues

Response Times

  • Critical Safety Issues: Within 2 hours
  • Content Violations: Within 24 hours
  • General Reports: Within 48 hours
  • Appeals: Within 72 hours

Enforcement Actions

  • Content Removal: Immediate removal of violating content
  • Account Warnings: Progressive warnings for policy violations
  • Temporary Suspensions: Time-limited account restrictions
  • Permanent Bans: For severe or repeated violations

Appeal Process

  • Fair Review: All enforcement actions can be appealed
  • Human Review: Appeals reviewed by senior moderation staff
  • Transparent Process: Clear explanation of decisions
  • Second Opinions: Option for additional review

üìú COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with dignity and respect
  • Use inclusive language and avoid discrimination
  • Respect different opinions and cultural backgrounds
  • Be kind and constructive in your interactions

Be Authentic

  • Use your real identity and information
  • Don't impersonate others or create fake accounts
  • Share original content and give credit where due
  • Be honest in your business transactions

Be Safe

  • Protect your personal information
  • Report suspicious or harmful behavior
  • Don't share inappropriate content
  • Respect others' privacy and boundaries

Be Positive

  • Contribute to a positive community environment
  • Share uplifting and educational content
  • Support other creators and users
  • Help build a thriving social commerce ecosystem

Commerce Guidelines

  • Provide accurate product descriptions
  • Deliver products as promised
  • Use secure payment methods
  • Resolve disputes fairly and promptly
